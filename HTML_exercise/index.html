Tom's Oatmeal Walnut Cookies!


Description

This is a recipe for super awesome, gluten-free oatmeal walnut chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 106g light brown sugar
  • 50g granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 1.5 tsp vanille extract
  • 156g gluten-free baking flour
  • 75 g quick cooking oats
  • 0.5 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp baking powder
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1 bag mini dark chocolate chips
  • 0.5 cup smashed walnut pieces

Preparation Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a microwave safe dish for 1 minute.
  2. Combine sugar, salt, vanilla, baking soda, and baking powder in a large bowl and mix thoroughly.
  3. Pour the melted butter into the bowl with the previously listed dry ingredients.
  4. Mix the previosuly listed ingredients thoroughly with the butter.
  5. Add the flour and oats, and egg. Mix thoroughly.
  6. Mix in the chocolate chips and walnut pieces (crush these to desired size in a zip-loc sandwhich bag.
  7. On two baking sheets, ball out the cookie dough in 1-2" dough balls. Space evenly, putting no more than 12 on a medium size baking tray.
  8. Bake the cookies for 13 minutes @ 325 degrees Farenheit.
